runik

• (ˈrü-nik)

a cross platform desktop app for generating and managing custom e-reader dictionaries.

Digital dictionaries for your favourite fictional worlds

Download

Enhance your reading experience!

Stay immersed in your book with in-text definitions for characters, places, and items unique to the world of your story. No wifi, no jailbreaking, no need to put down your device.

How it works

  • Provide runik the wiki url for the book or series you are reading.
  • Runik processes each wiki page, compiling them into a dictionary unique to your book.
  • Runik converts the dictionary and installs it onto your e-reader device.
