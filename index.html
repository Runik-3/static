runik

• (ˈrü-nik)

a cross platform desktop app for generating and managing custom e-reader dictionaries.

Generate custom e-reader dictionaries for your favourite fictional worlds

Download

How does it work?

  • Point runik to a wiki that contains info about the world of your book.
  • Runik processes the wiki pages into a lexicon of definitions unique to that world.
  • Runik converts the dictionary and sends it to your e-reader device.
